Tomato Mozzarella Salad

Ingredient

  • 3 large tomatoes, sliced
  • 8 ounces mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • ¼ cup olive oil
  • ¼ cup balsamic vinegar
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ¼ cup minced fresh basil

Directions

    Steps

  1. Place tomato slices, alternating with mozzarella slices,
    on a large serving platter.

  2. Combine oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper in a jar with a tight-fitting lid;
    shake well.
    Drizzle over tomatoes and mozzarella; sprinkle with basil.
